About the Artist

Hello, I'm Kyu, a self-taught freelance artist who has been passionate about drawing since my early childhood. Over the years, I've dedicated myself to honing my skills and continuously improving in the art realm. Drawing has been a constant companion in my life, and during my free time, I continue creating art to enhance my abilities and gain valuable experience.


Despite being self-taught, I remain persistent and never give up on my artistic journey. After four years of focusing on traditional drawing methods, I began witnessing noticeable improvements in my work. Intrigued by the popularity of digital art across various social media platforms, I decided to explore this exciting medium. I ventured into digital art using programs like Microsoft Paint and Adobe Illustrator for my initial creations, eventually adapting to the unique challenges and opportunities that digital techniques present.
