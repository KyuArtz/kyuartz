Frequently Asked Questions
Updated August 2025

What type of artwork do you specialize in?

I specialize in digital art, focusing on character illustrations, comics, and concept art. My style ranges from fantasy themes to modern designs, from vibrant to atmospheric pieces.

How does the commission process work?

Commissions operate on a first-come, first-served basis. After submitting your request, you'll be placed in the queue. I provide updates during the process.

What are your payment methods and policies?

I accept PayPal, Maya, and GCash. Payments can be 100% upfront or 50% upfront & 50% on completion.

Can I use your art commercially?

Art is for personal use unless a commercial license is arranged. Commercial use requires fees & attribution.

Do you offer refunds and revisions?

Up to two minor revisions are free. Refunds are only possible if I cannot complete the commission.
