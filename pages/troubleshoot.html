Troubleshooting Guide

If you’re experiencing issues while using KyuArtz, this guide will help you troubleshoot and resolve common problems. If your issue persists, feel free to contact support directly for assistance.

1. Website Loading Issues

Sometimes the website may not load correctly or parts of the site may not function as expected. Try the following solutions:

  • Clear your browser cache: Outdated cache files can sometimes cause loading problems. Clear your cache by going to your browser settings.
  • Update your browser: Ensure you’re using the latest version of your browser for the best experience. Older browsers may not support the latest web standards.
  • Disable browser extensions: Certain extensions (like ad blockers or script blockers) may interfere with the functionality of the website. Try disabling them and reloading the page.
  • Try a different browser: If the issue persists, test the website in a different browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari) to see if the problem is browser-specific.

2. Contact Form Issues

If the contact form isn’t working or you’re unable to submit a request, follow these steps:

  • Check your internet connection: Ensure that you have a stable connection. If the form doesn’t submit, it could be due to connectivity problems.
  • Double-check required fields: Make sure all the mandatory fields (such as your name, email, and message) are filled out correctly.
  • Reload the page: Sometimes refreshing the page can solve temporary glitches. After refreshing, try submitting the form again.
  • Use the direct email: If the form still doesn’t work, you can always email us at kyuushi992@gmail.com with your inquiries or commission requests.

3. Commission Form or Currency Converter Not Working

If you are having issues with the commission form or the currency converter, try these solutions:

  • Clear browser cache: Like the website issues, clearing your cache may help resolve problems with the form or converter.
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled: Both the commission form and currency converter rely on JavaScript. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser’s settings.
  • Try using a different device: If the form isn’t working on your current device, try submitting it using another device (e.g., mobile or tablet).

4. Image or Portfolio Page Not Loading

If images or portfolio pages are not loading correctly, here are a few potential fixes:

  • Check your internet speed: Slow connections can result in images not loading properly. Try refreshing the page once your connection improves.
  • Turn off ad blockers: In some cases, ad blockers may block images or content from being displayed. Disable the blocker temporarily to see if that resolves the issue.
  • Clear your cache: Cached images may fail to load if they’re outdated. Clear your browser cache and reload the page.

5. Mobile View Issues

Having trouble viewing the site on mobile devices? Here are some tips:

  • Refresh the page: If the layout doesn’t look right, refresh the page to ensure all elements load properly.
  • Try a different browser: If the mobile browser you’re using doesn’t support some features, try switching to another one, like Chrome or Safari.
  • Zooming issues: If you are having trouble with zoom, ensure that your mobile browser settings allow you to zoom in or out properly.

6. Payment Processing Issues

If you're having trouble with making payments or encountering errors during the checkout process, follow these steps:

  • Verify payment information: Double-check the payment details you have entered (e.g., card number, expiration date, billing address).
  • Use a different payment method: If one method isn't working, try using an alternate method (if available).
  • Contact your bank: If your payment is being declined, your bank might be blocking the transaction. Reach out to your bank to resolve the issue.

7. Other Technical Issues

If you’re facing other technical difficulties not listed here, please don’t hesitate to contact support with details about the issue. We’ll get back to you as soon as possible to help resolve the problem.
